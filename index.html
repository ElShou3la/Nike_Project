Web About

Nike is a multinational corporation that designs, develops, and sells athletic footwear, apparel, and equipment. The company was founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, and its headquarters are located in Beaverton, Oregon. Nike is one of the world's largest suppliers of athletic shoes and apparel, and its iconic "swoosh" logo is recognized around the globe. Nike's success is due in part to its innovative approach to product design and marketing, as well as its commitment to corporate responsibility and sustainability. The company has been at the forefront of many social and environmental initiatives, such as its use of recycled materials in product development and its support for various social causes. Nike has also been a pioneer in the world of sports marketing, with its endorsement deals for top athletes and its iconic advertising campaigns.
